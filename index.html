Top Ranked Engineering Colleges in India 2021

The economy of Punjab is the 14th largest state economy in India. There are numerous educational institutions in the state and has ample of opportunities for the students who wish to pursue BTech course. BTech Colleges in Punjab offer quality education with high placements. These colleges offer full-time BTech courses in Punjab that are your gateway to a career in Engineering. These courses are for a duration of four years. Of the many BTech specialisations, Computer Science Engineering is the most sought-after and offers bright prospects for studentâ€™s growth. Punjab is home to prestigious institutes to pursue a BTech course like Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Ropar; Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology, Patiala; Dr. B. R. Ambedkar National Institute of Technology (NIT), Jalandhar; Lovely Professional University (LPU), Jalandhar; I.K Gujral Punjab Technical University (PTU), Punjab; Sant Longowal Institute of Engineering and Technology, Sangrur; Chandigarh Engineering College, Mohali; Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College, Ludhiana, etc. In Punjab, BTech degree is offered across various specialisations. Some of the most prominent ones are as follows: Mechanical Engineering (Manufacturing Engineering) Computer Science and Engineering Computer Engineering Electronics and Communication Engineering Civil Engineering There are a total of nearly 135 engineering colleges in Punjab which offer a full-time and part-time mode of study. These colleges provide degree courses. Out of these, the top 10 engineering colleges in Punjab ranked by NIRF and India Today offer degree courses in a full-time mode of study. Some of these colleges offer dual degree specialisations. Chandigarh Engineering College, Mohali offers dual specialisation in Electronics and Communication Engineering.
